Cloudplot

Web Application

Client: Bitnapse Chile

The project

Many technical teams in building construction today still rely on emails to request the printing of project plans, technical specifications, and other critical documents. Emails often get lost or don't get a response, resulting in missed deadlines and money loss.

With CloudPlot, engineers upload their files, send printing requests, and track their advancement. The app automatically processes the logistics, sorting, delivery, and even team communications.
